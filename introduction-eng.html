Introduction about SaiGon Food Services

Operated as a unit under the field of providing industrial catering ration ( meal), we clearly understand the customer's food and beverage demand has been developing and being focused recently. Especially, the food service which provides the industrial meals to workers in firms, factories or even offers a meal to the office employees ( workers).

Simply put, industrial rations are pre-cooked rations and meals that serve a large number of users. According to a statistic, there are many industrial parks in the whole country but only a small number of industrial ranchers have full business licenses. The rest are small businesses. According to another survey by the National Institute of Nutrition in 2014, it was found that industrial or non-food diets were not sufficiently nutritious for the body to recover energy. And on the media there is also a lot of information about garbage picking or choosing the poor quality food for workers' meals for personal gain. This shows that the supply of industrial food is increasingly difficult to manage in terms of quality and food safety issues for meals in industrial zones.

In order to guarantee the quality and safety towards each food ration, we have been equipped with the most modern cooking system and facilities, which are able to intensive satisfy every standard of industrial catering. Moreover, Saigon Food Services responsibly creates and maintains the opportunities for our employees in terms of obtaining periodical health examination, as well as participating in the well-planned practical training of food processing methods.

Sai Gon Food Services keeps a promise of the quality, freshness, and equality in each ration when we delivery the dishes to our clients (customers) . In order to achieve our promise, we strictly execute our food process within the self-contained procedure and perform the cross-check stages from the raw material selection, ingredients input checking, preliminary food processing, food cooking, an output checking to ration dividing, packaging and finally transportation. The process and procedure are all performed under the Saigon Food standardized criterion. For us, the origin of food products must be clear, the shelf life of fresh produce must be completely new, the dry foods must be entered and the shelf life checked before processing. food ... how to ensure that the products to customers must be delicious and safe as the motto Sai Gon Food Services has set for the food "beyond the delicious is the guaranteed health "

Moreover, with the professional, enthusiastic kitchen team of Sai Gon Food Services, we are constantly innovating, always producing new dishes to help your meals more rich and diverse. We are constantly striving to provide people with delicious, nutritious and safe meals, helping people to have good health, full energy to complete their work.

Sai Gon Food Services specializes in providing:

  • Industrial catering for the company / enterprise
  • Provide lunch for office workers
  • Provides food for the office

With Sai Gon Food Services we are determined to say no to negative actions, ensure the input materials and quality output to bring meals to meet customer standards and safety for the health of users.
